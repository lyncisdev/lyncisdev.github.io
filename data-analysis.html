Improving credit collections in inner city Johannesburg

We helped a SME client analyse their existing customer data to improve their credit profiling and improve collections. We then automated the key reports using Microsoft Power BI so that they could up to date reports whenever they needed them.

Matching incomplete customer data

We developed a string matching tool in Python for a medium-sized South African bank looking to match incomplete or incorrect customer records. A key requirement of the project was that the model be able to estimate it’s own conference so that the client would be able to determine the applicability of the results. For example, a higher confidence threshold would be required of records used for credit scoring than for marketing.

Big data analytics for the banking sector

A large African bank asked us to help them develop customer insights for the credit division by consolidating data from various departments within the bank to create a more unified view of the customer. During the project we used used with the IBM Netezza data warehouse to analyse tens of millions of customer, transaction and other records.

Analysing mining efficiency

An African mine operator requested me to analyze data associated with it’s mining trucks (GPS records, odometer and engine operating time readings, operator logs, etc.) to identify proces bottlenecks and underutilized machinery that could be sold.
